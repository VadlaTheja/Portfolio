Intro

As a child, I was always fascinated by the stories behind numbers and patterns. This early curiosity has evolved into a deep passion for data engineering. I believe every dataset has a narrative waiting to be uncovered, and I thrive on transforming raw data into structured, accessible formats that drive informed decisions. My attention to detail ensures that no nuance is overlooked, while my problem-solving mindset pushes me to always ask "why" and dig deeper into data pipelines.

Currently pursuing my Master's in Applied Data Engineering, Business Analytics and AI atNaveen Jindal School of Management, UTD. I have built a solid foundation in data analysis, engineering, and machine learning. I am eager to apply advanced data engineering techniques to solve complex problems and ensure data integrity and efficiency. My aim is to decode data science for the real world in the simplest terms, making data pipelines robust, scalable, and impactful.
